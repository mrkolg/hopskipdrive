• School transportation
    • OVERVIEW

      HopSkipDrive partners with schools, districts and counties to provide a safe, reliable alternative school transportation solution.

    • DISTRICTS

      We partner with school districts — from large to small — to fulfill non-routine student transportation needs.

    • COUNTIES

      We work with counties to arrange school transportation for youth designated McKinney-Vento or in foster care.

    • CHARTER, PRIVATE & INDEPENDENT

      We partner with charter, private and independent schools all over the country to help them fulfill alternative transportation needs.

    • MCKINNEY-VENTO
      & FOSTER YOUTH

      We help organizations with school of origin transportation needs for youth designated McKinney-Vento or in foster care.

    • SPECIAL EDUCATION & IEPS

      We work with schools and districts to help with transportation for youth with special needs or those with transportation requirements in their IEP or 504.

    • ADDITIONAL TRANSPORTATION SOLUTIONS

      It doesn’t stop there — we also partner with nonprofits, continuing technical education, summer camps, extracurriculars and more!
