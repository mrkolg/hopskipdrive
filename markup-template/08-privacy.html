For all other Users, location information is only collected if the Application is running in the foreground.

We may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience within our Services . If you decide at any time that you no longer wish to accept Cookies from our Services for any of the purposes described above, then you can instruct your browser, by changing its settings, to stop accepting Cookies or to prompt you before accepting a Cookie from the websites you visit. Pixel Tags are tiny graphic images with a unique identifier, similar in function to Cookies, that are used to track online movements of Web users. In contrast to Cookies, which are stored on a user’s computer hard drive, Pixel Tags are embedded invisibly in Web pages. Pixel Tags also allow us to send email messages in a format users can read, and they tell us whether emails have been opened to ensure that we are sending only messages that are of interest to Users. We may use this information to reduce or eliminate messages sent to a User. We do not tie the information gathered by Pixel Tags to our Users’ Personal Information.

This policy does not apply to, and we are not responsible for, Cookies or pixel tags in third party ads, and we encourage you to check the privacy policies of advertisers and/or ad services to learn about their use of Cookies and other technologies. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here: http://www.aboutads.info/choices or http://www.networkadvertising.org.

  4. How we share information

    HOPSKIPDRIVE DOES NOT SELL YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION, AND WILL NOT IN THE FUTURE SELL YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION WITHOUT PROVIDING A RIGHT TO OPT-OUT. Your information may be shared with third parties as described below:

    • How we share Personal Information with Users. As you know, our Services are designed to connect Ride Organizers and CareDrivers for Rides. As such, if you are a Ride Organizer, we must share Personal Information about you and the Rider(s) to CareDrivers and if you are a CareDriver, we must share some Personal Information with the Ride Organizer(s) and Rider(s).
      • Rider and Ride Organizer Information Shared with CareDriver. Throughout the duration of the ride, the CareDriver will see the Rider information described in Section 3 above, and the name of the Ride Organizer, with certain protections in place. When claiming rides, CareDrivers see only the pick up and drop off locations. Only after the ride has been matched to a CareDriver will the CareDriver see any ride notes provided by the Ride Organizer. Only upon pick up will the CareDriver see the Rider’s information. Upon completion of the ride, Rider information is again masked in the CareDriver’s ride history following the ride. Once the ride is complete, we will eventually share the Rider or Ride Organizer’s rating and any feedback with the CareDriver. Although we remove the Rider or Ride Organizer’s identity when doing so, the CareDriver may be able to identify the individual(s) who provided the feedback. Rider and Ride Organizer phone numbers are masked via in-app communication. In the case of a lost or found item, we will seek to connect you with the relevant CareDriver or Ride Organizer. No actual contact information will be shared without your consent.
      • CareDriver Information Shared with Rider(s) and Ride Organizer(s). Once a CareDriver accepts a ride request, we will share with the Ride Organizer(s) and Rider(s) (if applicable) the CareDriver’s name, profile photo, short bio, and the vehicle make, model, color, and license plate.
      • Carpool and Referral Programs. If you initiate a Carpool ride or refer someone to the HopSkipDrive Platform, we will let them know that you generated the referral. If another User referred you or invited you into a Carpool, we may share information about your use of the Platform with that User. For example, a referral source may receive notification regarding participation in a Carpool or receive a bonus when you join the Platform. During any Carpool Ride each Rider and Rider Organizer may receive information about the other Rider(s) and Ride Organizer(s), including the Rider(s) and Ride Organizer(s) names, trip notes, and pick up or drop off locations for that Carpool Ride.
    • How we share Personal Information with Other Parties. In addition to the ways we share Personal Information as described elsewhere in this Privacy Policy, we may share Personal Information with other third parties in the following manners:
      • Payment Processing Information. For online payments and/or Automated Clearing House (ACH) payouts, we use the payment services of Stripe. We do not process, record or maintain your credit card or bank account information or other related information necessary to process payments. For more information on how payments are handled, or to understand the data security and privacy afforded such information, please see https://stripe.com/us/privacy.
      • Governmental Entities and Enterprise Clients. HopSkipDrive may be asked or required by certain applicable governmental entities or Enterprise Clients to report certain information about CareDrivers and your Rides for purposes of CareDriver licensure, approval, or required data reporting.
      • CareDriver Screening Providers and Resources. We work with background check, identity verification, physical exam (where applicable), onboarding, and other required or voluntary CareDriver screening providers. HopSkipDrive may make available certain tools and resources to CareDrivers for purposes of CareDrivers’ furthering their own independently established trades. CareDrivers who access or participate in these tools and resources may be required to share certain Personal Information.
      • Consultants and Other Business Service Providers. We may share your Personal Information with consultants and other business service providers. This information is provided in order for them to provide us critical services such as cloud storage, marketing and advertising services, data analytics, insurance and financing services, certain wheelchair or other specialty rides, and other services.
