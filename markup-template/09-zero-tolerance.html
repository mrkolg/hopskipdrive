HopSkipDrive is proud of its Zero Tolerance Policy against the use, possession, or being under the influence of drugs or alcohol while using the HopSkipDrive Platform. Any User found to have violated this Zero Tolerance Policy will have his or her HopSkipDrive account deactivated.

HopSkipDrive maintains a zero tolerance policy with regard to physical contact with riders other than for the reasons described in the Terms of Use or as requested by a caregiver for safety reasons. We also have a zero tolerance policy for the illegal use of a mobile device while driving.

Discrimination against passengers or drivers on the basis of race, color, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, physical or mental disability, medical condition, marital status, age, or sexual orientation is not allowed, and can result in deactivation from the platform.
