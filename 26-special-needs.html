The HopSkipDrive Difference

Flexible &
Cost-Effective

Our flexible, dynamic driver supply makes it easy to build out new routes or edit existing ones. We ensure that you will always fill rides, even with one day’s notice.

Driver supply increases and decreases with your needs; you’ll never pay for unfilled rides or a vehicle that sits empty.

Safe & Reliable

Safety is our #1 priority, from our 15-point CareDriver certification process to our Safe Ride Support system.

COVID-Safe Ride Standards are among the most stringent in the industry, crucial to immuno-compromised riders.

We know how important consistency can be to students with special needs. Rides are guaranteed; students will see an average of only 3 drivers in a 90-day period.

Caregivers on Wheels

CareDrivers must have 5+ years of caregiving experience — many were teachers, special education teachers and paraeducators, school administrators, social workers and more.

CareDrivers bring their experience, empathy and best practices for working with children into each ride.
